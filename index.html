About the Overseer

Elohe Ministries was founded by Apostle Shumba, a dedicated servant of God with a heart for the people and a vision for divine transformation. The church is located in Lusaka West, off Mungwu Road.

Services are held every Sunday at Mungwu Park, where believers gather to worship, grow in the Word, and experience the power of God.

Apostle Shumba is a spiritual son of Prophet Wiseman Daniel, who himself was nurtured under the leadership of Prophet T.B. Joshua at the Synagogue Church of All Nations (SCOAN).
